About Us

#INFLUENCED: See Beyond the Algorithm

#INFLUENCED is an educational microsite created to help students understand how social media algorithms shape what they see, what they click, and what they buy. This platform explains how algorithm-driven feeds work and why they have a powerful influence on purchasing behavior among college students.

Our goal is to raise awareness, promote critical thinking, and empower students to make informed online decisions. Through simplified insights, research-based information, and interactive visuals, this microsite breaks down complicated tech concepts into something easy and relevant to everyday life.

This project is part of the study "Social Media Algorithms Among College Students: Impact on Purchase Practice", aiming to highlight the unseen mechanisms behind digital influence and encourage mindful, responsible digital consumption.

Explore our content to understand how algorithms affect your choices—and how you can take control of your digital experience.
